Site Name

The name for the site will be "Rialto Chamber of Commerce", or rialtochamberofcommerce.com.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site will be to help local businesses grow by sharing their information with the local people and businesses and also spreading information about local events to the city.

Scenarios

Color Schema

My main color will be #F17105 orange with contrasting color #2D7DD2 blue, along with #29BF12 green to serve as additional colors. I plan on using #F5F0F6 magnolia for the main background of the whole site, with the orange as backgrounds to main body sections and the blue and green to serve as highlights to headings, points of interest, etc.

Typography

The two main fonts I will be using are 'Madimi One' and 'Encode Sans Expanded'. I plan on using Madimi One for the headings and Encode Sans for the main body.
