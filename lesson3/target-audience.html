The target audience for the Truesdale Chamber of Commerce would be the local farmers, small town businesses, and local markets.

Persona 1: Joel McNeal is a young farmer with a family of 5. He's been farming his whole life and has inherited his family's farm. While he doesn't have the education you would get in the city, he's an honest man who understands enough about the world to be wise about his life choices. He is currently looking into updating the family homestead with more modern technology to make farming easier and improve their quality of life.

Persona 2: Cynthia Forsythe is a single, middle-aged mother who is currently unemployed but looking to start her own business in order to keep busy. She is very tech savvy for her age and wants to start a business where she can teach technology classes to those in need, especially to the older generations. She plans on using the Chamber of Commerce as a launching point for her business and a place to scope out the local populace and their needs.

Scenario 1: Cynthia visits the website through her phone and wants to see what kind of businesses the locals prefer and which are flourishing.

Scenario 2: Joel is visiting the local library and uses the computers to visit the website. He wants to look into local businesses that sell more modern farming tools, such as tractors and livestock handling equipment.

Scenario 3: Cynthia's son suggested that her skills as a teacher could be used by other businesses in case starting her own doesn't work out. She wants to see what places could possibly use her skillset.

Scenario 4: Joel is looking to speak with other local farmers and wants to see if there are any local events or groups that he can meet with.

Scenario 5: In a chance meeting, Cynthia runs into the owner of a local supply chain, but fails to get their contact information. She wants to know if there is a way to reach them by finding their store in town.
