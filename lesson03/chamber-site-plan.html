Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Johannes Wendt

Site Name

The site's name will be 'City of Warrenton Chamber of Commerce'.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website will be to help local business owners to grow by promoting local events and opportunities while also growing our town through improving commerce and business.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find contact information for other business owners?
  • What local events are happening that I can promote my business at?
  • How can I find others willing to participate in a local business study?

Color Schema

The colors I have chosen for this site are:

  • #A28B55 as the main background
  • #86AB89 for headings
  • #CBE2B5 for other accents and highlights
  • #E7FBE6 for main body paragraphs

Typography

The fonts I will be using are:

  • Montserrat for the main body and general writing
  • Protest Strike for headings and hightlights
